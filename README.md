## About

This repository houses the living system of **Revolution of the Heart**—a framework for returning to oneself through curriculum, ritual, creative expression, and legacy work. Built intentionally for sustainability and accessibility, it grows organically to support trauma survivors and seekers of deeper presence.

---

## Structure

Navigate to each section of the work:

- **[Core (Trunk)](Core/)**  
  Philosophy, ethics, long-form vision, teaching frameworks.  
- **[reBElious ME](reBElious-ME/)**  
  Transforming survival into statement via writing, visuals, wearable art.  
- **[Everyday Angels](Everyday-Angels/)**  
  Intuitive messages, affirmations, symbolic storytelling for grounding support.  
- **[444-Generations Jewelry](444-Generations-Jewelry/)**  
  Legacy work honoring generational healing and continuity through vintage jewelry resale.  
- **[Courses & Education](Courses-Education/)**  
  Trauma-aware educational offerings for practical rebuilding and autonomy.

## Code of Conduct

This space is held with gentleness and respect. We ask that all contributors:

- **Honor trauma** — Approach this work with awareness that healing is not linear
- **Offer kindness** — Feedback is given with care; ideas are received with open hearts
- **Respect boundaries** — No unsolicited advice; let each person's path unfold
- **Celebrate diversity** — Many ways of healing are valid; honor different approaches
- **Stay authentic** — Share from genuine experience, not performance

If you experience harm or have concerns, please reach out to [contact below](#contact).
