# Discussions & Community

Welcome to the heart of our community. This is where we gather, share, and support one another.

## Discussion Categories

Our community is organized into these spaces:

### 💬 **General Chat**
Introductions, reflections, and everyday conversations. Meet fellow seekers and share what's on your heart.

### 🙏 **Blessings & Reflections**
Share gratitude, hope, encouragement, and personal reflections. Leave a blessing for someone walking a similar path.

### 💡 **Ideas & Feedback**
Suggest features, improvements, and directions for the project. How can Revolution of the Heart evolve?

### 📚 **Resource Sharing**
Share books, links, tools, and resources that support healing. Recommend what's helped you.

### 🤝 **Community Support**
Ask questions, offer wisdom, and support one another. No question too small; no experience too niche.

---

## Community Guidelines

We hold this space with care:

1. **Speak from the heart** — Share authentic experiences, not performance
2. **Listen with gentleness** — Honor others' paths; no fixing or advice-giving unless asked
3. **Respect privacy** — Don't share others' stories without permission
4. **Honor pace** — Healing is not linear; support different timelines
5. **No spam** — Keep conversations meaningful and in-service to the work

---

## Getting Started

1. Go to the [Discussions tab](https://github.com/FlowerofKindness/Revolution-of-the-heart-/discussions) on GitHub
2. Choose a category above
3. Start a new discussion or join an existing one
4. Share authentically—your voice matters

Need help setting up Discussions? See [SETUP-DISCUSSIONS.md](SETUP-DISCUSSIONS.md) for step-by-step instructions.
