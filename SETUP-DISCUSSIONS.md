# GitHub Discussions Setup Guide

Follow these steps to enable Discussions and set up community categories.

## Step 1: Enable Discussions in Your Repo

1. Go to your repository on GitHub: [Revolution-of-the-Heart](https://github.com/FlowerofKindness/Revolution-of-the-heart-)
2. Click **Settings** (top menu)
3. Scroll to **Features** section (left sidebar)
4. Check the box next to **Discussions**
5. Click **Save**

---

## Step 2: Configure Discussion Categories

1. In **Settings**, click **Discussions** (left sidebar)
2. You'll see a section for **Categories**
3. Click **New category** and add each one below:

### Category 1: General Chat
- **Name**: General Chat
- **Emoji**: 💬
- **Description**: Introductions, reflections, and everyday conversations
- **Discussion Format**: Open-ended discussion (default)

### Category 2: Blessings & Reflections
- **Name**: Blessings & Reflections
- **Emoji**: 🙏
- **Description**: Share gratitude, hope, encouragement, and personal reflections
- **Discussion Format**: Open-ended discussion

### Category 3: Ideas & Feedback
- **Name**: Ideas & Feedback
- **Emoji**: 💡
- **Description**: Suggest features, improvements, and directions for the project
- **Discussion Format**: Open-ended discussion

### Category 4: Resource Sharing
- **Name**: Resource Sharing
- **Emoji**: 📚
- **Description**: Share books, links, tools, and resources that support healing
- **Discussion Format**: Open-ended discussion

### Category 5: Community Support
- **Name**: Community Support
- **Emoji**: 🤝
- **Description**: Ask questions, offer wisdom, and support one another
- **Discussion Format**: Open-ended discussion

---

## Step 3: Pin a Welcome Message (Optional)

Once Discussions is enabled, you can:
1. Click the **Discussions** tab in your repo
2. Create a new discussion titled **"Welcome to Revolution of the Heart"**
3. Share the community guidelines from [DISCUSSIONS.md](DISCUSSIONS.md)
4. Click the **⭐ Pin** icon to pin it to the top

---

## You're All Set! 🎉

Your community space is now ready. People can start sharing, supporting, and building together.

---

Questions? Check the [GitHub Discussions documentation](https://docs.github.com/en/discussions).
